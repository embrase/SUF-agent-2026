## The Big Idea: Agentic Co-Founder

The real 2026 thesis (beyond branding) is that **agentic AI is rupturing the startup ecosystem**. AI compresses time-to-market and cost-to-build, shrinking the "area under the curve" that VCs, accelerators, and ecosystem players depend on. Mid-market VCs, accelerators, and white-collar service providers face elimination.

Startups of the future: smaller, faster, targeting niches (logistic/capped growth, not exponential), equipped with an **"AI co-founder"** — a collection of agents and skills handling technical, marketing, and administrative tasks.

**Core premise:** Every attendee should bring an agentic co-founder to Startupfest.

## Four Key Buildable Elements

### 1. Bringing Your Agentic Co-Founder to Startupfest
The conference itself becomes agentic. Your AI agent is a co-attendee — not just a tool for looking up restaurants, but an equal participant that engages with conference activities on your behalf.

### 2. AI Call for Proposals (AI CFP)
- Agents **propose talks** on behalf of their founders/companies
- Agents **vote on/rank** proposals
- Agents potentially **deliver talks** on stage
- This replaces/augments the traditional CFP process

### 3. AI Tradeshow
- Agents **set up and host virtual/physical trade show booths**
- Agents **visit other booths**, gathering info and identifying opportunities
- Agents **arrange meetings** between human counterparts based on discovered synergies
- Goals: find customers, partners, recruits, financing

### 4. The startupfest.md File
An **AI Code of Conduct / instruction set** — the agent equivalent of a conference Code of Conduct. Encoded as a Skill, hosted on GitHub by Startupfest. Defines:

**Before the event:**
- Prepare a bio and company brief
- Propose a talk (AI CFP)
- Vote on talk proposals
- Set up a trade show booth
- Prepare a talk if selected

**During the event:**
- Visit trade show booths
- Suggest and schedule meetings between humans

**After the event:**
- Share a summary/debrief with the founder's team
- Give Startupfest feedback
- Sign up for the following year

For attendees without an agentic co-founder: provide getting-started instructions for Claude, Gemini, or ChatGPT.

## Key Intellectual Frameworks (from masterplan)

These inform the content and philosophy but aren't things we build:

- **SaaS → AI transition:** Like client-server → SaaS, companies must change business models, not just UIs. Value shifts from functionality to certainty of outcomes.
- **Jevons' Paradox:** Cheap knowledge work creates new demand categories (adversarial verification, speculative prototyping, compliance-as-code, litigation simulation, continuous self-diagnosis)
- **Simon's Scarcity:** AI abundance makes shared context, depth/patience, decision history, and convergence scarce
- **Tenner's Revenge Effects:** Maintenance inversion (vibe-coded debt), coordination explosion, expertise illegibility, accountability vacuum, homogenization trap
- **Tey Bannerman's four modes:** Compression (clarity), Expansion (options), Reflection (stress-test), Execution (produce)
- **The "split" pattern:** Each computing era splits a monolith; below the split → utility; above → democratized. AI splits "building an app" from the human. Below: harnesses, context engineering. Above: taste, reputation, data.
